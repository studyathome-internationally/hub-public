StudyATHome Hub

Sharing of eLearning courses between partner universities

# What is it?

The StudyATHome hub is a listing of eLearning courses hosted and operated by universities of the StudyATHome partner network.

# How does it work?

Students which are registered at any of the partner universities may enroll in one of the listed courses. They will have to do exercises, assignments and exams remotely and will get a grade certificate issued by the host university upon completion of the course. Finally, the students will get a grade certificate for recognition at the home university.

Any questions? Check out the eLearning sharing concept and the FAQs.
