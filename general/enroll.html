The course enrollment requires your matriculation first. Please send an email containing your matriculation data to the International Office of the University holding the course by clicking Compose E-Mail below.

Matriculation Data

  • Course Name / Link
  • Personal
    • Name, Surname (Family name)
    • Date of Birth
    • Sex
    • Nationality
    • Address
    • University
  • A-Levels (University Entrance Qualification)
    • Date
    • Country of Awarding Institution

If you are studying a Master degree, please also provide that entrance qualification (Bachelor degree).

Master Entrance Qualification (Optional)

  • Date
  • Country of Awarding Institution

Then you will get an email containing the matriculation receipt and the course login information.

Please ensure to enroll before the matriculation deadline.

Matriculation Deadline

January, 26th 2020
